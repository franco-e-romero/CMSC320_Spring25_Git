Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: We may consider using Git because it allows for version control, collaboration, and tracking changes over time.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: One field that data science could apply to is finance. To be specific, data science can be used to analyze market trends, assess risks, and predict stock prices.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: The difference is that merging preserves the commit history with a new merge commit, while rebasing creates a linear history by reapplying commits.
